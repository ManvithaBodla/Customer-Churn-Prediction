# Business Insights

The analysis of the customer churn dataset provides the following insights:

- Customers with month-to-month contracts are more likely to churn.
- Customers paying higher monthly charges show a greater tendency to leave.
- Customers with longer tenure are generally more loyal.
- Fiber optic internet users exhibit relatively higher churn.
- Customers using electronic check as a payment method have higher churn rates.

## Recommendations

- Offer discounts to customers with month-to-month contracts.
- Introduce loyalty rewards for long-term customers.
- Improve customer support for high-risk customer groups.
- Provide personalized retention offers to customers likely to churn.
